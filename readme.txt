The pioneer WooCommerce Free Product Sample plugin for WooCommerce based e-commerce website. Promote your business in minutes!

== Description ==

WooCommerce Free Product Sample plugin is the right choice to offer to order as free sample product to customer in specific date duration, powered by WooCommerce.

= HOW IT WORKS? =
* Store Owner will be able to offer to order some product as free sample 
* Order a Free Sample button will be displayed in product detail page

== Key Features ==

* Button can be added in simple and variable product both.
